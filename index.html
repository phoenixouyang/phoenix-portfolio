About Me

I'm a software developer who loves building products that make life easier. What hooks me is the problem-solving — taking something messy and complex and turning it into something that just works.

Before making the switch, I spent five years as a Financial Analyst at companies like Ubisoft, Brookfield, Lindt, and Unilever. I enjoyed analyzing products — but I wanted to be the one building them. So I quit, completed a Computer Programming Diploma, and started shipping. You can see what I've been building below.

Outside of coding, you'll usually find me baking, reading, running, gaming, or traveling. I make fantastic cookies, and I'm a great teammate to have if you ever find yourself trapped in an escape room.
